Most Popular Dishes

Idli & Chutney

A classic South Indian combo — idli & chutney! 🥥🍛 Idli: Soft, fluffy steamed rice cakes made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram). Super light, easy on the stomach, and naturally gluten-free. Chutney: Usually coconut-based, but there are tons of variations:

Jalebi

jalebi — now we’re talking sugar spiral heaven! 🍯🔥 Golden, crispy on the outside, juicy and syrupy inside — made by deep-frying fermented maida batter in circular swirls and soaking them in saffron-cardamom sugar syrup. Sweet, sticky, and just melts in your mouth.

Plain Thali

Plain thali — the OG comfort meal. 🍽️✨ A classic Indian thali keeps it simple but balanced — usually served on a steel plate with small katoris (bowls), hitting all the flavor notes: salty, spicy, tangy, and sweet Optional sides: chutney, salad, and sometimes a small bowl of buttermilk

Paneer Tikka

Ahhh yes, paneer tikka – the vegetarian rockstar of tandoori grills!Chunks of paneer marinated in a spicy, tangy yogurt-based masala, skewered with bell peppers and onions, and roasted or grilled to smoky perfection.Tandoor or oven-roasted – it never disappoints.
